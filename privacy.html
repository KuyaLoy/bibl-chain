Bible Chain Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) is intended to assist the user (“You,” “Your”) in understanding how the Bible Chain (“Platform,” “We,” “Us,” “Our”) collects, uses, and shares the your personal information in order to help you better understand and utilize your privacy rights.

This Privacy Policy applies when you visit https://bibl-chain.com ("Site"), use our Bible Chain DApp (“App”), contact our customer service team, engage with us on social media, or otherwise interact with us in any other way. The terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms of Services, accessible at Bible Chain unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may revise this privacy policy at our sole discretion. Changes to this Policy will be announced on social media or on the Site. If we make changes, we will notify you by updating the top of this Policy or through other means such as adding a statement to our website or sending you a notification. We recommend that you check this Privacy Policy on a regular basis to keep informed.

If you disagree with the revised section, we urge you to stop using Bible Chain right away. Otherwise, your continued browsing on the Site and future websites owned or managed by Bible Chain imply that you accept the new Policy.

Personal Information We Collect

Bible Chain collects information that you provide to us directly when taking part in a survey, donation, and other events; requesting customer support; or otherwise communicating with us when you provide certain information. Personal information we may collect includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

A) Information We Collect Automatically

  • We may collect information automatically when you visit or interact with our Site and App. It includes transaction details, mobile device and usage information, location information (you may decline the collection of precise location data at any time), and some other relevant information.
  • We may collect information from you through cookies or similar technologies when you visit our site. Cookies are small files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that collect visitor behavior information. If you do not allow cookies to be stored in your browser, you can generally prevent their use.

B) Information We Collect From Other Sources

We may collect information from third-party sources and organizations. For example, we may collect information about you that you have made available via your privacy settings when accessing our site through a third-party application.

Use of Information

We may use the information we collect from you for a variety of purposes. These include:

  • Providing and improving our Platform as well as rendering our services to you - This includes developing new features, analyzing how you use our Platform, and providing customer support.
  • Marketing and advertising our products and services. We may use your information to send you advertising messages or other promotional materials and customize the ads you see when you use our Platform.
  • Processing transactions and sending you related information (e.g., confirmations, receipts, invoices, customer experience surveys, etc.).
  • Protecting our customers, employees, and business. We may use your information to detect and prevent fraud, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
  • Complying with legal obligations. We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, warrants, court orders, and other legal processes.

Disclosure of Information You Provide

We may disclose personal information as permitted by, or required by, any applicable law or legal process, including lawful requests from government agencies to fulfill national security or law enforcement demands.

We may also share your information with other organizations for various reasons, including major business transactions such as a merger, sale, or asset transfer.The disclosure of your information is pursuant to your consent.

Security of Your Information

When you provide personal information on our platform, we employ a variety of security measures to secure your data and guarantee that it is not lost, misused, or tampered with.

Your Privacy Choices

You have the privacy choices about your personal information, which are determined by applicable law, as well as:

  • Cookies

    • Cookies are set to be accepted by default in most web browsers. If you wish, you may adjust your browser settings to limit, delete, or refuse browser cookies. Please keep in mind that if you clear or reject cookies, our site may not be accessible.

  • Communications Preferences

    • You may unsubscribe from getting emails from Bible Chain by following the instructions in those newsletters.

Third-Party Applications / Websites

Links to other websites/applications, as well as other websites/applications themselves, may be present on our website. We do not manage these third-party services. Users are encouraged to read the privacy policies of each website and application with which they interact before providing personal information.

Bible Chain does not endorse, screen, or approve of any third-party websites or applications and are not liable for their privacy practices or content. It is your own responsibility to give out personal information to other websites or applications.

Contact Us

If you have any queries regarding our Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights as set out in it, kindly contact us at:support@bibl-chain.com
